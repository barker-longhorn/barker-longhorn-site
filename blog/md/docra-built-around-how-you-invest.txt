This month we're showcasing the features that sit at the heart of how it works: modularity and customisability.

Every investor has a different process. Different thesis, different stage focus, different checklist. Most tools ignore that and hand you a fixed workflow. DOCRA starts from the other direction: you define what good looks like, and the software applies it.

That means you can upload your thesis and instantly see how any startup stacks up against your own criteria, not a generic scoring rubric. It means your data room checklist reflects your standards. And it means the templates you build today are reusable across every deal you run tomorrow.

The goal is a tool that gets sharper the more you use it, because it's learning your process, not teaching you a new one.
